DAGOZILLA ITB

About

  • Founded in 2016, DAGOZILLA ITB is a research team from Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB) which focuses on Mobile Robot Development.
  • Co-founded and built a strong multidisciplinary engineering team.
  • Developed vision system with OpenCV in C++ for Mobile Soccer Robots to detect ball, field, lines, and obstacles. Also initiated catadioptric mirror transformation feature.
  • Guided the team until in 2020 to be qualified for RoboCup Middle Size League for the first time (https://msl.robocup.org/news/2020-qualification-results).
