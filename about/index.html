My Story

In 2016, me with other colleagues founded DAGOZILLA ITB, back then still a Mobile Soccer Robot Team that competes annually in a local wheeled soccer robot competition called KRSBI-B (Kontes Robot Sepak Bola Indonesia Beroda). After graduated from Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB) in 2018, I’m still giving advices and helped the team become more established. After 4 years of research and development, by 2020, we’re qualified to compete in RoboCup Middle Size League for the first time at the good rank for a new comer. Besides Mobile Soccer Robots, DAGOZILLA ITB also developed Telepresence Robot that can controlled remotely and has ability to make video calls.

Since October 2018, I’m working as Backend Developer for Bukalapak. On the first year in the company, I was assigned to BukaPengadaan, an e-procurement solutions for businesses, and involved in various features there. Then, I was assigned to a company-wide project called Gatotkaca to move our on-prem services to the cloud. Now, I’m assigned to help develop some features in Mitra Bukalapak - our O2O product - and BukuMitra - bookkeeping and business promotion tools for SMEs.
