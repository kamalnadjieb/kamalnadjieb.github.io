My Story

In 2016, me with other colleagues founded DAGOZILLA ITB, back then still a Mobile Soccer Robot Team that compete annually in a local RoboCup Middle Size League-typed competition named KRSBI-B (Kontes Robot Sepak Bola Indonesia Beroda). After graduated from Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), I still gave advices and helped the team become more established. After 4 years of research and development, by 2020, we’re qualified to compete in RoboCup Middle Size League for the first time at the good rank for a new comer. Besides Mobile Soccer Robots, DAGOZILLA ITB also developed Telepresence Robot that can controlled remotely and has ability to make video calls. Please contact us via instagram or email if you want to support our work :)

I’m currently working as a Backend Developer at Bukalapak, one of the largest e-commerce company in Indonesia backed by some notable investors (Microsoft, Standard Chartered). First, I was assigned to BukaPengadaan, a B2B e-commerce, and involved in various features there. Then, I was assigned to a company-wide project called Gatotkaca to move our on-prem services to the cloud. Now, I’m assigned to help develop some features in Mitra Bukalapak, our O2O product.
