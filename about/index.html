Short Story

I’m currently working as a Backend Developer at Bukalapak, one of the largest e-commerce company in Indonesia.

In 2016, me with other colleagues founded DAGOZILLA ITB, back then still a Mobile Soccer Robot Team that compete annually in a local RoboCup Middle Size League-typed competition named KRSBI-B (Kontes Robot Sepak Bola Indonesia Beroda). After graduated from Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), I still gave advices and helped the team become more established. After 4 years of research and development, by 2020, we’re qualified to compete in RoboCup Middle Size League for the first time at the good rank for a new comer. Besides Mobile Soccer Robots, DAGOZILLA ITB also developed Telepresence Robot that can controlled remotely and has ability to make video calls.
